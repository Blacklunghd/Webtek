Crash in Mid-Air

Überlingen Collision

Backstory

On the night of 1 July 2002, BAL Bashkirian Airlines Flight 2937, a Tupolev-T 154 jet, and DHL Flight 611, a Boeing 757 cargo jet.

collided in midair over Überlingen, a southern German town on Lake Constance, near the Swiss border.

All of the passengers and crew aboard both planes were killed, resulting in a total death toll of 71
